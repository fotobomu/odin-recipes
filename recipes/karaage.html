Karaage Chicken

Delicious, delicious, chicken!

Ingredients

  • Chicken
  • soy sauce
  • mirin
  • sake
  • potato starch
  • vegetable oil

Directions

  1. Cut chicken into pieces.
  2. Mix chicken with soy sauce, mirin, sake. Let marinade for 1 hour.
  3. Dredge chicken in potato starch.
  4. Fry chicken pieces in hot oil.
  5. Serve with lemon.
